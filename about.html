Who Is Phantom Millz?

I'm Phantom Millz, a horror-focused content creator who loves bringing people together through gaming, community events, and complete stream chaos.

Whether I'm exploring terrifying worlds, surviving impossible situations, or letting chat influence my fate, every stream is about creating memorable moments and having a laugh along the way.

The goal has never been to build an audience. The goal has always been to build a community.

👻 Community First

The Phantom Legion is a community built around friendship, support, and fun.

🎮 Gaming Variety

From horror games to survival adventures, no two streams are ever the same.

💀 Interactive Chaos

Expect viewer interaction, community challenges, and cursed decisions.

The Mission

To create a place where people can escape reality for a few hours, laugh together, and become part of something bigger than a stream.
