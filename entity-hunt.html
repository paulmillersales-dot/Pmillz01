👁️ What Is Entity Hunt?

Entity Hunt is a community investigation event where chat works together to identify a mysterious entity hiding within the Phantom Realm.

Unlike Boss Hunts, Entity Hunts are not about dealing damage. They're about observation, deduction, and uncovering the truth.

📜 Step 1

An unknown entity enters the Phantom Realm.

🔎 Step 2

Clues begin appearing throughout the stream.

🧠 Step 3

Use the clues to determine which entity is active.

How To Guess

When you think you know which entity is active, use:

!hunt Entity Name

Example:

!hunt The Broadcast

If you're correct, the hunt ends and the entity is identified.

⚠️ Beware False Assumptions

Some clues may be obvious.

Some clues may be subtle.

Some clues may deliberately point in the wrong direction.

The entities do not always want to be found.

📺 The Broadcast

Distorted transmissions and corrupted signals.

🎭 The Marionette King

Strings, puppets, manipulation and control.

🌑 The Shadow Entity

Darkness, whispers and unseen movement.

🏆 Why Participate?

Every successful hunt expands the Entity Archives.

The more entities discovered, the more secrets of the Phantom Realm are revealed.
