👁️ What Is Entity Hunt?

Entity Hunt is a community investigation event where chat works together to identify a hidden anomaly active within the Phantom Realm.

Unlike Boss Hunts, Entity Hunts are not about dealing damage.

Instead, researchers must analyse clues, patterns and strange activity to determine which anomaly has appeared.

🔎 Investigate

Watch chat carefully and gather clues as they appear.

🧠 Deduce

Use the evidence to determine which anomaly is currently active.

📜 Identify

Submit your answer before the mystery is solved.

How To Participate

During an active hunt, clues will appear throughout the stream.

These clues may reveal behaviours, patterns, motives, or characteristics of the active anomaly.

Use the hunt command to submit your answer.

!hunt [Anomaly]

Example:

!hunt WATCHER

Research Notes

Not every clue should be trusted.

Some anomalies actively attempt to mislead investigators.

Others leave almost no evidence at all.

Researchers are advised to compare all available information before reaching a conclusion.

🏆 Why Participate?

Successful investigations expand our understanding of the Phantom Realm.

Every anomaly identified helps strengthen the archives and improve future containment efforts.

Some anomalies are common.

Some are extremely rare.

Some remain poorly understood...
