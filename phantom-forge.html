What is The Phantom Forge?

The Phantom Forge is where the community submits ideas for custom 3D prints, stream props, creepy creations, and chaotic little projects.

Each month, viewers vote on the submitted ideas. The winning creation is then brought to life on stream.

How It Works

  1. Redeem The Phantom Forge - Submit for Voting using channel points.
  2. Submit your idea for the monthly vote.
  3. The community votes on the best idea.
  4. The winning idea is made on stream.

Channel Point Redeems

Submit for Voting

Cost: 15,000 channel points

Submit an idea to be included in the monthly community vote.

Custom Forge Request

Cost: 125,000 channel points

A more direct custom creation request, reflecting the time, effort, and materials involved.

Submission Rules

  • Ideas must be stream-safe.
  • No copyrighted models unless permission is available.
  • Overly complex builds may be adjusted to fit time and materials.
  • Final approval sits with Pmillz01.
