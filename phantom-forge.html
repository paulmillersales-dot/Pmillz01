⚒️ What Is The Phantom Forge?

The Phantom Forge is a community-driven creation project where viewers can submit ideas for custom 3D prints, stream props, decorations, horror-themed creations, and other chaotic inventions.

Each month, the community votes on submitted ideas. The winning project is then brought to life on stream.

  • Very large or complex projects may be simplified.
