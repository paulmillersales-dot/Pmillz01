What Is The Phantom Forge?

The Phantom Forge is a community-driven creation project where viewers can submit ideas for custom 3D prints, props, decorations, horror-themed creations, and chaotic inventions.

Each month, the community votes on submitted ideas. The winning project is then brought to life on stream.

How It Works

Viewers submit their ideas through the Phantom Forge channel point redeem. The community votes, the winner is chosen, and Pmillz brings the creation to life.

Forge Rules

All submissions must be stream safe, realistic to make, and suitable for the community.

Very large or complicated projects may be simplified. Final approval always rests with Pmillz01.
