📖 Description

The Mirror Thing is one of the least understood entities within the Phantom Realm.

It appears through reflections, mirrored surfaces, camera feeds, and occasionally through objects that should not be capable of reflecting anything at all.

Researchers disagree on whether the entity exists independently, or if every sighting is merely a reflection of something much worse.

⚙️ Known Behaviour

The Mirror Thing has repeatedly demonstrated the ability to reverse outcomes.

Actions intended to harm it may instead strengthen it. Attempts to heal may cause damage. Expected results should never be assumed during an encounter.

The entity appears to delight in confusion, contradiction, and uncertainty.

Witnesses frequently report seeing movements in reflections that did not occur in reality.

⚠️ Containment Notes

  • Do not trust mirrors during active containment.
  • Do not wave at your reflection.
  • If your reflection waves first, containment has failed.
  • If a reflected version of yourself begins speaking independently, do not respond.

🔬 Research Findings

The Mirror Thing has never been observed directly.

Every confirmed encounter has occurred through a reflective medium.

This has led some researchers to question whether anyone has actually seen the entity at all.

Others believe that question is exactly what the entity wants us to ask.
