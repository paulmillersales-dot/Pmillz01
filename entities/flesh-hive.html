ENTITY FILE: TFH

🩸 THE FLESH HIVE

Biological Hive Entity • Regenerative Growth • Corrupted Matter

🩸 Entity Image

Threat Level

Severe

Classification

Biological Hive Entity

Containment Status

Biohazard Containment

Description

The Flesh Hive is a biological entity formed from corrupted growth, shared instinct, and hostile regeneration.

It does not behave as a single creature. Instead, it functions like a living network, spreading through damaged tissue, failed containment zones, and anything foolish enough to offer it strength.

Known Behaviour

The Flesh Hive becomes significantly more dangerous when strengthened. Healing attempts and offerings may cause rapid biological expansion.

In containment events, the entity has been observed doubling the effect of restorative actions, growing stronger from attempts that would stabilise other entities.

If the walls begin breathing, leave the area immediately.

Containment Notes

Do not feed the hive.

Do not touch exposed growth.

If you hear multiple voices speaking from the same mouth, containment has already failed.

Archive Connection

All recorded appearances, defeats, and containment events involving The Flesh Hive are logged automatically through the Phantom Realm archive system.
