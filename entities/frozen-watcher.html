📖 Description

The Frozen Watcher is a silent observational entity associated with stillness, cold spaces, delayed movement, and prolonged eye contact.

It rarely acts first. Instead, it watches.

Researchers have reported sudden drops in temperature, frozen screen effects, slowed reactions, and the overwhelming feeling that something is waiting for them to make the first mistake.

⚙️ Known Behaviour

The Frozen Watcher is unusually resistant to weaker attacks.

During containment events, minor strikes may fail entirely, as if the entity simply refuses to acknowledge them.

Its stillness should not be mistaken for weakness. The entity is most dangerous when nothing appears to be happening.

⚠️ Containment Notes

  • Do not blink repeatedly while observing the entity.
  • Do not stand still for extended periods in cold zones.
  • If frost appears on the inside of your screen, terminate observation immediately.
  • If the entity turns its head, containment staff should evacuate.

🔬 Research Findings

The Frozen Watcher does not appear motivated by hunger, violence, or devotion.

It observes until conditions become favourable.

No researcher has confirmed what happens when it finally decides to move.
