📖 Description

The Marionette King is a hostile control entity associated with strings, puppetry, false movement, and forced behaviour.

Victims often report feeling as though their choices were made before they made them. During encounters, actions may feel delayed, redirected, or strangely rehearsed.

⚙️ Known Behaviour

The Marionette King is capable of avoiding attacks by manipulating the conditions around the strike, making successful containment unusually unpredictable.

It is believed to pull at probability itself, causing attacks to miss, timing to fail, and researchers to question whether they acted freely at all.

If laughter is heard from above, containment teams are advised to stop moving.

⚠️ Containment Notes

  • Do not follow strings.
  • Do not look up if you hear wood creaking.
  • If your limbs move before you decide to move them, report immediately.
